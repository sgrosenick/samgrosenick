Hey there, I'm Sam!

I'm a cartographer and prospective graduate of the UW-Madison GIS and Web Map Programming program. I live and work in Anchroage, Alaska and love to spend my freetime outside in the mountains. My cartographic interests are mainly focused on creating terrain maps and interactive maps, but I'm always interested in making new types of maps.

If you like the maps I have made and would like me to make one for you, or just want to talk about maps, feel free to contact me.
