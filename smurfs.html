Smurftastic Tileset

This was a fun Smurf-themed map that I had fun making. Using CartoCSS and Mapbox Studio Classic, I was able to take the 80's cartoon themes such as bright greens and blues, along with very bubbly text to show the world like you'd see it on the Smurfs. My favorite part of the map is the use of mushrooms to symbolize cities.
