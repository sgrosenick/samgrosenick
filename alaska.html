The Great Alaksan Cycling Race

The Great Alaskan Cycling Race map is a ficticous map I made for a Graphic Design in Cartography class I took. It was a chance to play with Adobe Photoshop with the goal of making a beautiful terrain map. I utilized a digial elevation model (DEM) and land cover data in Photoshop to combine the two datasets. Here I used blending tools to find just the right level of blending between the two so that the reader can see the topographic detail of the DEM while at the same time seeing the color of the land cover, giving the whole map a realistic look.
